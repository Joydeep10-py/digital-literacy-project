## 🌟 The Scenario
As a newly selected **Student Digital Ambassador**, my mission is to guide my peers through the complexities of the digital world. This portfolio serves as a comprehensive resource for professional online presence, secure communication, and cyber-resilience.

---

## 🛠️ Project Modules

### 📍 Module 1: Visual Awareness
* **Deliverable:** Digital Literacy Infographic.
* **Core Topics:** Safe Internet Practices, Email Etiquette, and Professional Branding.
* **Tools Used:** Canva / Generative Design.
* **Highlight:** Designed a high-fidelity visual guide to help students secure their digital footprint instantly.

### 📍 Module 2: Professional Identity
* **Platforms:** LinkedIn, GitHub, and Kaggle.
* **Objective:** Building a foundational digital portfolio that speaks to recruiters and the academic community.
* **Focus:** Optimized the GitHub Profile README and LinkedIn education sections for maximum professional impact.

### 📍 Module 3: Technical & Collaborative Mastery
* **Coding Skill:** Completed the **Python (Basic) Certification** on HackerRank.
* **Collaboration:** Designed a custom **Google Form Quiz** to assess digital literacy levels among peers.
* **Practice:** Mastered the workflow of cloud-based data collection and technical skill validation.

### 📍 Module 4: Communication Etiquette
* **Professionalism:** Drafted formal academic and corporate emails (NVIDIA & VIT Faculty).
* **Digital Ethics:** Established a **10-point Social Media Checklist** to help students maintain a clean digital reputation.
* **Skill:** Focused on subject line clarity, tone, and structured sign-offs.

### 📍 Module 5: Cyber-Resilience Guide
* **Case Study:** In-depth analysis of **UPI "Collect Request" Fraud** in the Indian context.
* **Resource:** Developed a "Stay Safe Online" checklist covering 8 actionable prevention tips.
* **Emergency Contact:** Included the National Cyber Crime Helpline (**1930**).
